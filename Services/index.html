What You Can Expect


Personalized Music Education

Astronote offers tailored lessons in theory, instruments, vocals, and composition. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced musician, our flexible learning options help you grow at your own pace.

Professional Music Services

From live recording to mix mastering and music transcription, Astronote provides high-quality services to enhance your sound. Our expertise ensures your music is polished and industry-ready.

Artist Growth & Opportunities

Develop a standout portfolio, access exclusive courses, and connect with professionals. Astronote helps musicians expand their skills, gain visibility, and take their careers to the next level.

Music Lessons & Training

We offer a wide range of music lessons that cover different aspects of music, including theory, instrument proficiency, vocal training, and composition. These lessons are tailored to each student's needs and are available both in person and online. Additionally, we provide specialized training to help students develop advanced skills in specific areas of music. This may include training in particular genres, production techniques, or performance skills.

Flexible Learning Option

Astronote offers students the flexibility to learn at their own pace, with the option to choose between online or in-person classes based on their preferences. Each session lasts one hour, with one lesson per week to ensure consistent progress. Currently, online classes are delivered through third-party platforms like Microsoft Teams and similar services, offering a seamless virtual learning experience.

Live Recording & Mix Mastering

Astronote offers professional Live Recording services, allowing musicians to capture high-quality sound during live performances or studio sessions, either in-person or virtually. Once recorded, our Mixing & Mastering service ensures the final track is polished and cohesive. Mixing blends individual elements like vocals and instruments, while mastering enhances the overall sound for consistency across all platforms and playback systems. This comprehensive service helps artists achieve a professional-quality release.

Music Transcription

Astronote offers music transcription, converting compositions into written or digital formats. This service helps musicians, producers, and composers easily share, study, or perform their music, and is essential for creating sheet music or analyzing a song's structure.

Portfolio Development for Music Artists

Portfolio Development helps musicians build a professional representation of their work. This can include creating a digital portfolio with recordings, performances, music videos, and other assets that showcase their musical style and talent. Astronote helps artists develop portfolios that are attractive to potential clients, record labels, and fans.
